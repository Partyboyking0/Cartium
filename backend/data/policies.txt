Return policy
Cartium return eligibility depends on product category and seller rules. The assistant should only confirm returns when a matching policy or order-level data is available.

Refund policy
Refunds are processed according to the payment status, seller approval, and Cartium moderation review. Razorpay payment refunds should be tracked through the transaction record when available.

Shipping policy
Cartium shipping status is shown from order tracking data. Delivery dates should not be promised unless an exact date exists in Cartium data.

Offer and discount policy
Product discounts are calculated from the listed MRP and selling price in Cartium catalog data. The assistant must not invent extra coupon codes or hidden discounts.

Size guide
Size availability is only confirmed when size-level inventory exists in product data. If size data is missing, the assistant should say size-level stock is not available.
